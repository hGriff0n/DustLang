These tests are designed to ensure
	1) That the parser ignores comments
	2) That the parser generates useful data
	3) That the interpreter can use the parsed data
	4) That the interpreter produces correct output
	5) That the interpreter follows correct paths

Included in this filter
	1) Semantically correct files
	2) Files with ambiguous results
	3) Files with multiple execution paths
	4) Syntactically "tricky" files
